ABOUT US


The Be Dauntless Foundation is India’s Proactive Youth-Led Organisation with a vision of reimagining a better world for the underprivileged children in society. The foundation aims to awaken, acquaint, and exhort rural communities about the importance of quality education and therefore is determined to enhance, empower, and educate the youth of today for a cohesive tomorrow.

TESTIMONIAL'S

Dauntless made me believe in myself. I never thought that I would be able to continue with my studies. It is believed in my family that girls should get married at an early age. With the support of Dauntless, I'm able to build confidence within myself to stand up for the right.

- Poonam from 9th grade



I'm thankful to Dauntless for showing me the path through education to get a better livelihood. For a long time, I’m working as a plumber to fulfill my family’s necessity. Dauntless helped in building my ability to stand out.

- Arvind from 7th grade



I always faced gender discrimination in my family, we're 4 sisters and 1 brother. My father always shows his unhappiness because he had so many daughters and only one son. One of my younger sisters died early. Every time my brother always gets the best facilities and clothes. He was the centre of my mom and dad’s lives, I felt so left out. After joining Dauntless, I learned about how education is important in life to fulfill your dreams. I’m the most awarded student at Dauntless with the title of "Student of the Day.

- Nandini from 5th grade
